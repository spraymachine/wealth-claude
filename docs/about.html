A studio for regional ambition.

We believe India is not one market. It's twenty-two. Wealth Media Agency is the studio built to serve all of them — with cultural fluency, operational depth and receipts.

Mission

Turn brands
into authority.

Wealth Media Agency is a results-driven digital marketing and branding agency focused on helping businesses grow online. We specialise in social media marketing, content creation, branding and performance-driven campaigns that build strong visibility and real engagement.

Our goal is simple — turn brands into authority and attention into growth. We pair disciplined performance thinking with the cultural intuition of a local creative agency, across eight languages and twenty-two states.

A short history.

Built slowly, then suddenly. Here's the arc so far.

2019
Founded in Hyderabad

Started as a regional influencer desk with three people, one brief, and a rented room above a bakery.

2021
Regional expansion

Telugu + Tamil + Kannada networks built out. First seven-figure performance campaigns across Meta & Google.

2023
Offline division launched

Metro, bus, LED, gated-community and retail placements — owned end-to-end, national coverage.

2026
Twenty-two states. Eight languages. Still counting.

A full-stack marketing studio serving founders, CMOs and category leaders — with every campaign traceable to a KPI.
